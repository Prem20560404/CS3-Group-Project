Enjoy a Stress-free Job Search

Being international students in the US, finding jobs in companies that are willing to sponsor our work visas after graduation is a major problem that we all face together. We are required by law to go back to their home countries upon completion of their college education or find a company willing to sponsor them for a change-of-status which itself is a lengthy process and costly. Work Connect helps us:

1. Work Connect makes it easier for students who fall in this category to find companies in the United States that would be usually willing to take them up for sponsorship.

2. Users will be able to type in what type of company they would like to work with, and get results with information about companies that have job roles in the field based on their input.
